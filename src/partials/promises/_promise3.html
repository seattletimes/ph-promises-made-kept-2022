Promise #3
Bring 500 people in off the streets of Seattle by the end of 2021
Source: King County announces new proposal to bring 500 homeless residents into shelter using federal stimulus funds
Promise in progress
In April of 2021, King County Executive Dow Constantine announced a goal to bring 500 people living unsheltered in Seattle into shelter by the end of 2021.
According to the county, less than half of the goal has been met.
