Promise #6
Buy 3 New Apartment Buildings
Source: Seattle to help buy three new apartment buildings to house people facing homelessness
Promise in progress
In September of 2021, the city of Seattle said it would help purchase three brand new apartment buildings to house people who are homeless. It said that the buildings would be occupied by the end of 2021.
Two out of the three were purchased in 2021.
The Boylston Apartments
420 Boylston Ave. E
Seattle, WA 98102
Broadway Apartments
506 10th Ave. E
Seattle, WA 98102
Harvard Apartments
225 Harvard Ave. E
Seattle, WA 98102
But no one moved in by the end of the year.
